std::queue< BlockMovement > generate_block_positioning_order ( const std::vector< BlockPose > &  blocks,
const std::unordered_map< Block, BlockPose > &  targets 
)

Generates dependencies between the positioning of the blocks.

Some blocks could be into the target position of another block.

Parameters
blocksThe initial positions of the recognized blocks.
targetsThe desired block target positions.
Returns
A queue of BlockMovement with the order of movements in order to avoid conflicts.
Exceptions
std::invalid_argumentIf blocks contains more than one block of each type.
planner::ConflictingPositionsExceptionIf two blocks are initially too near to perform safely the picking of any.
planner::DeadlockExceptionIf it is impossible to perform movements due to circular dependencies.
